🌪️ Vortex Discord Rules 🌪️ 🙌 Maintain Respect: Ensure all General Chats are polite and respectful. 🚫 Avoid Spamming: Do not engage in spamming, racism, or harassment in any channel. 🚫 Prohibit Harassment: Any form of harassment, doxing (including threats), or behavior that is homophobic, transphobic, xenophobic, racist, sexist, or pedophilic is strictly forbidden, even if presented as a joke or meme. General trash-talking is acceptable. 🚫 No DDOS, Hacking, or Glitching Discussions: Refrain from discussing DDOS, hacking, or glitching outside of report tickets and bug reports. 📢 No Server Advertising: Promoting other servers in-game or within the Vortex Discord is not allowed. 🚫 No Mass Tagging: Refrain from mass tagging users. 🚫 Avoid Tagging Staff: Do not tag staff members unnecessarily. 🛡️ Comply with Discord's TOS: Do not discuss topics that may violate Discord's Terms of Service. ⚠️ Discourage Toxic Behavior: Creating toxic situations or debates to undermine other players or staff members is unacceptable. 🚫 No False Tickets: Do not create false tickets. 🔠 Appropriate Nicknames: Nicknames must not contain invisible, unreadable, or unmentionable characters that hinder chat interaction. 📩 No Direct Messaging Staff: Direct messages to Vortex Staff are prohibited. Use the ticket system in <#1333540622677708820> instead. All direct message requests will be ignored. Most of these guidelines are based on common sense.
